For Thermostat control, You can lower to a minimum of 60 degrees or a maximum of 90 degrees before recieving an energy alert on the screen. 
For Light Control, You can drag slider left to right to simulate light brightness. 
For Camera Control, You can toggle the Camera On/Off for privacy/security purposes.
